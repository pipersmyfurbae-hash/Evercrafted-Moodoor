Honest answer to the obvious question

Can I actually build this?

If you can hold floral wire and follow a layering order — yes. The blueprint does the part that takes years to learn: where things go, and why.

  • Placement is given in clock positions, not florist jargon
  • The build guide sequences every stem, so order is never a guess
  • Shopping lists include substitution notes when a stem is hard to find
  • Most builds take an unhurried afternoon
