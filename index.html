Why it holds up

Every wreath begins as a blueprint, not a vibe

Behind each design is a scored composition — balance, rhythm, proportion, contrast, silhouette — placed on a polar grid before a single stem is cut. The emotion is interpreted. The geometry never is.

Asymmetry by design

Weighted clusters and counterweights — never a centered blob, never mirror symmetry.

Directional movement

Greenery extends outward along believable lines of growth, with room left to breathe.

Buildable, every time

Stem counts, attachment points, and layering order are part of the design — not an afterthought.
